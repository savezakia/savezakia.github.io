About

I got discharged from hospital. My heart pressures, pulse and breathing was somewhat under control now. But I was no more able to do any of my household chores. Not even cutting an apple.
Nadir took a few days off from office to take care of me day and night. Another week passed taking full rest but I was not able to live up normally to my everyday routine. That day it broke me from inside when I saw Nadir in kitchen doing dishes. I forgot the promise of staying strong, and cried like a baby.
I murmured that I would never be able to get back to my life, to be able to do my household again, and to be able to be a helping hand for my husband. He came to me and tried to assure that everything is going to be fine. He said, "You just need to take little more rest and some extra care, inshAllah you will get better.
"But this time, my doubtful heart was not ready to accept what he was saying. "Your life is getting tough because of me", I added. He looked at me, smiled and said "You know! As a matter of fact, all the easiness and brightness of my life is because of you."
